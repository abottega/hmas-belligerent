Crew Member ID: 005

Mara Yousser

SCIENTIST

Age: 46

Bio:

To those in the field of mineral science, Mara needs no introduction. She is highly acclaimed as one of the pioneers of experimental research techniques and was one of the co-creators of the Magnotron excavator, which mining behemoth BHP have commercialised in their sector. This milestone achievement earnt Mara the cover of Time Magazine in 3009. The invention of this revolutionary excavator also sent the Plough Jones stocks soaring, and has made Mara a multi-billionairre in the Crypto Stock world. Whilst most researchers would retire comfortably with this many credits in the bank, Mara continues to put her name forward for new deep space mineral research missions, stating "If I don't do it then someone else will probably screw it up".
