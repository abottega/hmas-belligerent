Crew Member ID: 007

Freddy Sonett

BOTANIST

Age: 31

Bio:

Freddy spent his entire childhood growing up on a farm on Ceres IV. His father was a zucchini fluffer, and his mother was the town prostitute. It's no wonder he took a liking to the field of Botanism at an early age. Completing his studies at the Albert R. Broccoli University on Ceres IV, Freddy was assigned to the 'New Colonists' movement and spent 9 years setting up Air Purification and Edibles at New Berlin, Scuti VII and Beren. Freddy now joins the HMAS Belligerent for his first deep space research mission.
